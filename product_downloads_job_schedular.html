Download Argent Job Scheduler

The Argent Job Scheduler consists of two components – the Argent Queue Engine where the jobs actually execute, and the Argent Job Scheduler that schedules jobs to the various queue engines.

Both components are in the same zip file. Both products take a total of about three minutes to install.

While Argent’s products take only minutes to install, it took a team of Argent engineers over six months to make the installation this quick and easy – ‘God is in the details’.

The Argent Job Scheduler is capable of scheduling in excess of 500,000 jobs per day and supports up to 10,000 queue engines.

Argent Job Scheduler 2401-A (32-bit and 64-bit)

Argent Queue Engine 1809-A and above needs NEW key, Old keys are no longer supported. Contact Support@Argent.com.
