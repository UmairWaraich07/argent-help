Download Argent WorldView

Argent WorldView is a real-time facility based on Web 2.0 technology that integrates with all your Argent Job Scheduler installations

Argent WorldView syncs with all your Queue Engines local / remote and will show the live status of Jobs. With drill down feature you can go to any Job instance from a single location. You can even hold the Jobs in no time

Integration with Argent BoardRoom will allow you to display the entire Argent WorldView in a large screen display

Argent WorldView brings the power and freedom of web technology to manage your business better

Argent WorldView is compatible with Argent Job Scheduler 10.0A 1601-A and above
